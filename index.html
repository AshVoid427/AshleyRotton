Prosopagnosia AI: Developing an Assistive Device for Face Blindness

Competition: Florida Atlantic University Wave Competition

Year: 2025

Idea: This project is being developed as part of the 2025 Wave Competition, aiming to create an innovative solution for individuals with Prosopagnosia (face blindness). The AI-powered device utilizes neural networks and Raspberry Pi technology to help recognize and identify.

Goal: The goal is to improve independence and enhance the quality of life for individuals who struggle with facial recognition, providing them with a reliable assistive tool.
